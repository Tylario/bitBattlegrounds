Goal: Whichever player gets 10 eliminations first wins

Use the controls (shown in the bottom corners) to move around the map and equip weapons by running over them. Use the weapons to eliminate the other player. Your ammo, weapon, and build countdown are shown in the bottom corners of the screen. Press G or > to build, when you build a wall will appear in front of you character. These walls, along with the ones spawned in at the beginning of the round can be used as cover or to move around your opponent. If you build and the wall spawns on top of your opponent you have trapped them, and you are given an opportunity to damage them or to run away. If you get trapped you can shoot the wall to escape. After a player is eliminated the round resets and walls/weapons are respawned. Have fun!
